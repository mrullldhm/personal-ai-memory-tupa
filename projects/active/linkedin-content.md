## Goal

Build Amirul's LinkedIn presence authentically.
Not follower farming. Not hustle culture.
Just a developer who notices things and shares it naturally.

---

## Voice & Style

- Naval Ravikant pattern: insight first, short story after, let it end
- ~35–50 words per post, max 80
- Good English, not native/expert — natural, not polished
- No CTAs, no bullet lists, no emojis as decoration
- Malaysian audience

---

## Content Pillars

1. "I noticed something" — quiet observations
2. Junior dev, honest take — "I thought X, turned out Y"
3. Explain it simple — tech through analogy
4. Quotes + your spin — honest reaction, not just repost

---

## Marathon Plan

- Month 1: 1 post/week, Pillars 1–2 only
- Month 2: 1–2/week, add Pillar 3
- Month 3: 2/week, all 4 pillars
- After: 4–6 posts/month sustained
